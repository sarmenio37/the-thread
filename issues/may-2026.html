No big vacations on the horizon this year, which is a bummer. We're dreaming of Costa Rica and Disney, but they're on the back burner for now. Next spring, I'm planning a Florida trip with my high school friends for our big 4-0. Also, a game day party for my birthday is in the works, so stay tuned for details. No pressure to come, but you'd both obviously be invited!
