Sarah
📍 Cleveland, Ohio

✨ Highlight: Survived a travel whirlwind, salsa nights, and a toddler bed upgrade — all in June!

June was a whirlwind, to say the least! It kicked off with my company’s main conference, communityLive, in Orlando, where I led a panel and gave presentations. The hustle and bustle were worth it, and I managed to connect with some fantastic customers. However, my travel luck took a nosedive soon after — from flight cancellations to a red-eye drive from Detroit, it was chaos. But hey, I made it back home just in time to repack for Krakow, Poland. The travel gods weren’t done with me yet, as my bag decided to play hide and seek across continents. Still, I got to enjoy Polish cuisine and connect with coworkers worldwide. Pierogies and Polish beer? Yes, please!

Back home briefly before jetting off to Puerto Rico for the hiMSS conference, where I danced salsa into the night with coworkers. It was a month packed with trips, and Chris deserves a medal for holding down the fort with Vela. Speaking of Vela, she's now the proud owner of a big girl bed, and we passed her crib onto Jule and Dewey for their little one. We also enlisted our neighbor to tackle those nagging house projects that never seem to get done. This last week is all about catching up with family, taking Vela to the zoo with Kyla and Irie, and enjoying some downtime. Chris is off too, redoing the porch while Vela continues to splash about in her beloved swimming lessons. All in all, a wild, wonderful month, with Cookie's birthday shenanigans and breakfast with my sisters and mom to top it all off.

A note from all three of us

As June waves goodbye, we're left with a delightful irony: while Caroline and Kyla have vanished into their own mysterious adventures, Sarah's whirlwind of travel reminds us how sometimes the best journeys are the ones we don't document. Here's to thriving in chaos, whether on a silent escapade or a Polish pierogi tour. Until next month, may our inboxes be full and our updates, well, existent.
